How PIPBench works

Four steps from a PDF you didn't ask for to a memo you control.

STEP 01

Property intake

Brand, flag family, keys, chain scale, state, and what triggered the PIP — sale, refi, or renewal.

STEP 02

Paste your PIP

Upload or paste the scope table. PIPBench parses each line and sorts it against the industry-standard renovation categories (the Nehmer/HVS taxonomy).

STEP 03

Benchmark with confidence grades

Every category is checked against sourced cost data, escalated to 2026 dollars, with tariff-sensitive lines flagged.

STEP 04

Negotiation memo

What to accept, what to question, what to counter — each with the sourced evidence behind it.

VERIFIED

The base figure comes straight from a named primary or strong secondary public source.

DIRECTIONAL

Interpolated, escalated, or drawn from a lower-confidence source — shown as an estimate, not a fact.

DIRECTIONAL Figures based on the 2021 Nehmer/HVS Guide are escalated 25–35% to approximate 2026 dollars, derived from post-COVID PIP cost increases plus Turner/RLB indices. No direct 2021–2026 PIP-specific escalation index exists publicly, so every escalated figure carries the Directional grade — never presented as a sourced fact.
