# Naming & Domain — PIPBench

## Requirements
- Contains "PIP" — the ICP (franchise hotel owners) knows the term cold, and the winnable SEO cluster is "PIP cost" queries (`design/icp-market-channels.md` §3.6).
- Signals benchmarking/evidence, not guru-ism — direct contrast with PIPGURU ("The PIP Whisperer") and PIPGENIUS; our brand is measurement, not mystique.
- .com available (check only — nothing registered, per guardrails).

## Decision: **PIPBench** (pipbench.com)
- Says exactly what it does: benchmark your PIP. Zero explanation needed for the ICP.
- "Bench" carries three aligned meanings: *benchmark* (the product), the *surveyor's benchmark* (a physical verified reference point — the logo seed), and *workbench* (a tool, not a consultancy).
- Collision check: web search for "PIPBench"/"pip bench" software returns only unrelated Python packaging tools (frappe-bench, pybench, etc.) — no company, no hospitality usage, no trademark surfaced in search. [Note: a proper USPTO/trademark counsel search is a launch-plan step; not performed in this run.]
- Nameplate style: **PIPBench** (caps PIP, capital B) — "PIP" reads as the industry acronym it is.

## Tagline
**"Your PIP isn't a bill. It's an opening offer."**
- Chosen over descriptive alternatives ("The evidence-first PIP benchmark", "Know what's fair. Prove it.") because it reframes the entire category in nine words: competitors sell you *a number*; PIPBench changes what the document *is*. It is also unmistakably owner-side — no GC, brand, or consultant would say this.
- Secondary/functional line for meta and footer: *"Benchmark every line of your PIP against named public cost sources — and take the memo to the negotiation."*
