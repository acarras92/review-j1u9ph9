Start with the property

Enter your property, its key count, and its service tier — the benchmark ranges retune to the tier you pick (economy through luxury, plus extended-stay). Then paste your PIP scope & budget on the next step. Fields are pre-filled with a fictional sample; overwrite them with your own.

Paste your PIP scope & budget

This is where your scope and budget go. Paste the PIP scope table or contractor bid into the box below — PIPBench parses each line, extracts the price, and maps it to a benchmark category.

Format: one line per scope item, each ending in its cost — e.g. Guestrooms — soft goods, casegoods, lighting (141 keys) ......... $6,455,346. Every line needs a $ amount (a line without one is skipped). Use Load sample to see the exact format, or Clear to start from scratch.

The benchmark

Every line is measured against a published range, escalated to 2026 dollars. Green band = benchmark range; the marker is this bid. VERIFIED = primary-sourced base figure. DIRECTIONAL = interpolated or escalated — shown honestly as an estimate.

The negotiation memo

Negotiation support, not legal or engineering advice. Escalations to 2026 dollars are PIPBench estimates and are labeled as such.
