# Tournament Entrants — Run 1

Eight entrants: the four seeded candidates, the two scout-surfaced ideas, and two designed combinations. Judges must ground every score in the evidence files in `run-1/research/` (including the gap-fill files) — not in general knowledge.

## E1 — Change-Order Audit (seeded C1, standalone)
Owner-side SaaS for hotel renovations: upload a change order, it flags missing backup documentation (labor hours, material invoices, sub quotes), benchmarks pricing against market cost data, and drafts the pushback/documentation request. Buyer: hotel owner / asset manager mid-renovation. Evidence: `candidate1-change-order-audit.md`.

## E2 — PIP Bid Benchmark (seeded C2, standalone)
Benchmarks a brand-mandated PIP bid line-by-line against market cost ranges so a franchise hotel owner knows what's fair before signing. Buyer: independent/franchise hotel owner facing a PIP. Direct competitors: PIPGURU, PIPGENIUS. Evidence: `candidate2-pip-benchmarking.md`.

## E3 — Draw & Lien-Waiver Tracker (seeded C3, standalone)
Verifies monthly contractor payment applications (G702/G703) against percent-complete and tracks lien waivers across sub tiers for hotel owners. Evidence: `candidate3-draw-lien-tracking.md`.

## E4 — GC Track-Record Dossier (seeded C4, standalone)
Public-records-only reports on a GC's litigation, mechanic's-lien, and licensing history, hospitality-focused. Direct competitor found: Business Screen. Evidence: `candidate4-gc-track-record.md`.

## E5 — FF&E Reserve / Capital-Needs Forecaster (scout A, standalone)
Standardized, self-serve hotel capital-needs forecasting: models actual FF&E/capex need by plant life-cycle and building age vs the 3–5%-of-revenue reserve formula, flagging underfunding before it becomes a brand-standard failure or loan-covenant problem. Buyers: owners/asset managers AND lenders/CMBS servicers. Analog category with proven WTP: HOA/condo capital reserve studies. Evidence: `new-candidate-scout.md`.

## E6 — Capex Defense Combo (C2 + scout A)
Continuous hotel capex intelligence for owners: reserve-adequacy forecasting year-round, plus PIP bid benchmarking at the PIP moment. One subscription spanning the recurring need (reserves) and the acute need (PIP). Buyers: owners/asset managers; lenders as second market.

## E7 — Construction Defense Combo (C2 + C1 + C4-lite)
The owner's defense suite across the renovation lifecycle: PIP bid sanity-check before committing; GC public-records background pull at contractor selection; change-order audit + pushback drafting during construction. One buyer, three moments of the same project.

## E8 — Soft-Displacement Underwriter (scout B, standalone)
Models "soft displacement" revenue loss (guests avoiding a property under renovation) that standard renovation underwriting excludes. Included for completeness; the scout itself assessed the evidence trail as too thin. Evidence: `new-candidate-scout.md`.
